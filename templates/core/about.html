Our Story

Started small.
Built things that work.

AI-Solutions was founded in Kathmandu by Pratik Rauniyar with a single conviction: AI is only as good as its adoption. A brilliant model that sits unused is worth nothing.

Our first project was a simple chatbot for a local healthcare clinic. It handled 60% of patient enquiries automatically within the first month. That result — concrete, measurable, experienced by real staff — is the kind of outcome we've been chasing ever since.

Today we work with organisations across Nepal and the UK, delivering AI solutions in healthcare, retail, logistics, education, security, and finance. We're a small team that moves fast and stays close to every client.
