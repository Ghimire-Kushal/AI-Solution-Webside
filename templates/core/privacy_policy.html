Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. This policy explains how AI-Solutions collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Who We Are

AI-Solutions ("we", "us", "our") is a technology company specialising in AI-powered workplace automation, headquartered in Chester Road, Sunderland, UK, SR1 3SD, with an upcoming UK office in Sunderland, England. Our email address is student.helpline@sunderland.ac.uk.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, and message content when you use our contact form or email us directly.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on the site, browser type, device type, and approximate location (country/city) via anonymised analytics.
  • Chatbot interactions: Messages you send to Aria, our AI assistant, to generate a response. These are not stored permanently.
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for full details.

We do not collect payment information, sensitive personal data (health, ethnicity, etc.), or data from children under 16.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide support.
  • To send you information about services you have requested.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where applicable, we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests: To respond to enquiries and improve our services.
  • Consent: For non-essential cookies (you can withdraw consent at any time).
  • Contract: When processing data to fulfil a service agreement.
  • Legal obligation: Where required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months, after which they are permanently deleted. Analytics data is retained for up to 24 months in anonymised form. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 7).

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • OpenRouter / Meta LLaMA: Powers the Aria AI chatbot. Messages are processed in real time and not retained by us beyond the session.
  • Google Maps: Embedded maps on our Contact page. Google may set cookies when you interact with maps.
  • Bootstrap CDN / jsDelivr: Serves CSS and JavaScript libraries from a content delivery network.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy and we recommend reviewing them directly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict processing of your data.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at student.helpline@sunderland.ac.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Transfers

Our servers are based in Nepal. When we use third-party services (such as OpenRouter), your data may be processed outside Nepal or the UK/EEA. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: student.helpline@sunderland.ac.uk
  • Address: Chester Road, Sunderland, UK, SR1 3SD
